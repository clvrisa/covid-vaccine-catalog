The Pfizer–BioNTech COVID‑19 vaccine sold under the brand name Comirnaty is a COVID-19 vaccine developed by the German company BioNTech in cooperation with American company Pfizer.It is given by intramuscular injection. It is an RNA vaccine composed of nucleoside -modified mRNA (modRNA) encoding a mutated form of the spike protein of SARS-CoV-2, which is encapsulated in lipid nanoparticles. The vaccination requires two doses given three weeks apart.
